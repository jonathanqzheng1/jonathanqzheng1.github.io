Overview

  • The main study contains a series of similar studies, each series contains several small batches of translation pairs. We will release each series one by one. This is the first series of studies.
  • For this series, there are 3 studies on Profilic for you to finish. You need to finish all 3 studies to finish the current series. In other words, you need to submit 3 times on 3 different Thresh pages.

Payment

  • We will closely monitor the overall performance, and you will receive feedback for each series.
  • In addition to the base payment you will receive on Prolific, you will also receive a bonus payment for each series if you can maintain your annotation performance above a threshold.
  • Meanwhile, you will also be awarded for being active in communications with us. For instance, if you have a disagreement with our feedback, feel free to message us on Prolific.
